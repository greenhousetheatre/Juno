A performance venue. A community space. A Greener Fringe.
The Greenhouse is a zero-waste performance venue at the heart of the Edinburgh Fringe. Creating and hosting 8 shows – from storytelling to family theatre, dance to straight drama – all inviting you to discuss the environment, and our relationship to it. Our programme of workshops provide a fun and practical space to explore implementing sustainability in to artistic practice or daily life. Environmental events plus live music every night creates a relaxed space to unwind and share thoughts with other artists. This project is about a sense of community – and generating grassroots, artist-led change towards a more sustainable future. So what are you waiting for? Come on in.
