Writing for the Theatre
August 13-15
15:00–15:30

Tropical marine ecosystems possess the highest diversity and abundance of epibenthic invertebrates that contain zooxanthellae. Dinoflagellates in the genus Symbiodinium represent the most common group of these photosynthetic endosymbionts, and their presence contributes substantially to the productivity, survival, and success of their hosts.
