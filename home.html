A performance venue. A community space.
A Greener Fringe.
The Greenhouse is the first ever zero-waste performance venue at the Edinburgh Fringe. With 8 shows, an exciting programme of workshops, and live music every night, it is a practical space dedicated to exploring how sustainability and the arts can work together. Through our environmental initiative and sustainability events, we will create a space for like minded artists to meet, share, and grow. This project is about generating grassroots change through the arts towards a more sustainable future. So what are you waiting for?
Come on in.
